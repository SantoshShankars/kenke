kenke
=====
Kenken is a famous AI problem with constraints. My first attempt to solve the problem with python. 
USes backtracking with and without heuristic.

Input.txt
5
1 - 0 5
4 . 1
9 + 2 6 7
24 * 3 4 8
12 + 9 14 19
2 - 10 15
2 / 11 16
3 - 12 13
10 * 17 18
12 + 20 21 22
2 / 23 24

Output
1 4 5 3 2
2 3 1 4 5
5 2 4 1 3
3 1 2 5 4
4 5 3 2 1
